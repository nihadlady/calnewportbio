Biography

Cal is an MIT-trained computer science professor at Georgetown University who also writes about the intersections of technology, work, and the quest to find depth in an increasingly distracted world. Between 2016 and 2021, Cal published three culture-shifting, bestselling books that dissected the rise of digital distraction at work and at home, and provided frameworks for combating these diversions.
